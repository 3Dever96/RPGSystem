Base Skills
	Block: Block incoming attacks from the front.
		Controls: Hold B

	Sense: Detect nearby treasures and secrets.
		Controls: N/A

	Warp: Return to the last safe location visited.
		Controls: Hold R and press A

	MP Regen: Restore Magic Power over time.
		Controls: N/A

	Surge: Charge up and throw a magic sphere.  The sphere does damage while touching enemies.  Charging the sphere continually costs MP.
		Controls: Hold and Release R

	Nullify: When afflicted by negative status effects, this skill will periodically attempt to remove the affect.
		Controls: N/A

	Crouch: Crouch down.  While crouched, user can pass through lower walkways and can better sneak around enemies.
		Controls: Hold LZ

	SP Regen: Restore Stamina Power over time.
		Controls: N/A

	Sprint: Increase movement speed at the cost of SP.
		Controls: Hold RZ

	Scan: Reveal the HP of targeted enemies.
		Controls: N/A

	Attack: Perform a melee attack combo with the user's weapon.
		Controls: Press X repeatedly to chain combo moves

	HP Regen: Restore Health Power over time.
		Controls: N/A

Tier 1:
	Reflect: Adds the ability to reflect projectile attacks using the Block skill.
		Controls: N/A

	Weapon Bless: Infuse the user's weapon with magic energy to temporarily raise the attack power.
		Controls: Hold R and Press X

	Air Walk: Delays the effect of gravity and maintain jumping capabilities for a short time after walking off a ledge.
		Controls: N/A

	Magic Satellite: Summon three magic orbs that rotate around the user, protecting from damage and dealing damage to enemies upon contact.
		Controls: Hold R and Press B

	Vortex: Gives the Surge skill the effect of drawing enemies towards it.
		Controls: N/A

	Cloak: Temporarily makes the user invisible and undetectable by enemies.
		Controls: Hold LZ and Press R

	Loot Magnet: Draw dropped items toward the user.
		Controls: N/A

	Sneak Attack: Zip between enemies dealing little damage.  The user is invulnerable during this technique, but each usage consumes SP.
		Controls: Hold LZ and Press X

	Quick Engage: If the user is out of attack range when engaging enemies, the user will toward the targeted enemy.
		Controls: N/A

	Double Jump: The user can jump while in mid air.
		Controls: Press A while airborne.

	Intimidate: As the user continues to deal damage to enemies, the enemies become nervous and eventually flee the battle.
		Controls: N/A

	Reversal: The user can flip around their opponent to attack from behind.
		Controls: Hold B and Press A
